CYBERSECURITY

Everyone's at risk

We put more and more trust into our devices each day and it's becoming increasingly vital to ensure the safety and security of our digital information. Cybersecurity is the protection of information as well as systems from the countless cyberthreats they are exposed to daily. From malware and ransomware to phishing attacks and exploit kits, your information has never been at more risk.

October is cybersecurity month and UCT's CSIRT is giving away a free iPad to raise awareness of the threats of cyberattacks.
